Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a supplier? ▼
Click "Join as Supplier" in the navbar or visit the supplier registration page. Fill in your business details, set a password, and submit. Our team will review your application within 1–2 business days.
Is it free to list products on TradeHub PK? ▼
Yes — listing your business and products on TradeHub PK is completely free. We do not charge commissions on deals made between buyers and suppliers.
How long does supplier verification take? ▼
Supplier verification typically takes 1–3 business days. Once approved, your profile receives the ✅ Verified badge, which increases buyer trust and inquiry rates.
How do buyers contact suppliers? ▼
Buyers can contact suppliers directly via WhatsApp using the 💬 button on supplier profiles, or by sending an inquiry through the platform. There are no middlemen.
Can I list products from multiple categories? ▼
Yes. Once you're a verified supplier, you can list products across different categories from your supplier dashboard. There's no limit on products you can list.
