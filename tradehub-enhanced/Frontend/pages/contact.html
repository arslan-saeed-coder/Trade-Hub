Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? ▼
Browse our products, click "Buy Now" on any item, fill in your delivery details, and place your order. You'll be redirected to WhatsApp to confirm it with us directly.
How do I track my order? ▼
Log in and visit "My Orders" to see the status of every order you've placed, from Pending through to Delivered.
Do I need an account to order? ▼
No — you can check out as a guest. Creating an account just makes it easier to track your order history.
How do I contact you about an order? ▼
You can reach us directly via WhatsApp using the link sent after checkout, or through this contact form.
